Our Story

The Young European Biotech Network (YEBN) was founded in 2002 with the mission to create a collaborative and supportive environment for young professionals and students in the life sciences and biotechnology fields across Europe.

Our Goals

CONNECT

Connecting life sciences students, young professionals, and their associations for collaboration.

EMPOWER

Contributing to life sciences students’ and young professionals’ career development and growth.

ADVOCATE

Identifying shared needs and problems, working together to find solutions, internationally.
